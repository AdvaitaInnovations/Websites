Services

Workshops

We conduct film making workshops for aspiring film makers and actors periodically.We conduct corporate and college film making workshops as well.Our workshops aim at giving an overall idea about both the creative and technical aspects of film making.

Corporate Ads

We take up corporate ad-film projects which includes both shoot and post production.We can execute the ideas provided by the clients or we can provide creative inputs in conveying the ideas based on the clients requirements.

Short Films

If you have an idea and are looking for a team to make the movie , we can help you with different aspects of the film , right from early stages like scripting,screenplay writing to the post production aspects like editing and sound design for the film.

Post Production Services

We even take up only the post production aspects such as editing,titling,subtitling,poster design,sound design and color correction for short-films ,ads and other videos.

Promos / Videos

We take up promo shoots,trailer shoots,making videos for short films and feature films.We also do portfolio video shoots and editing for actors based on the requirements.

Goldenagefilmhouse Weekend film making workshop

Goldenagefilmhouse Weekend film making workshop

In 2 days, we not only teach you about film making , but also help you make your first short film .

Aspects covered : Basics of Screenplay writing|Story boarding|Casting|Acting|Direction|Cinematography|Editing|Dubbing|Music

Along with a theory session about various aspects of film making,there will also be a hands-on session where the team will come up with an idea for a film, shoot it and edit it during the course of the workshop,so as to give the participant, an overall understanding of how a film gets made.

A very good platform for aspiring actors and film makers to learn the basics of all the aspects of film making and also to meet like minded folks.

Selected folks from the workshop will get to be a part of the future projects of Goldenagefilmhouse team.

About Instructor

Vihaan (Keerthi Natarajan)

Vihaan is a national level award winning actor/writer/director and editor who has worked in around 40 shortfilms/adfilms and around 5 Kannada feature films till now.
He is the founder of the film making company GoldenAgeFilmHouse.
His short film 'Navilugari' won at Bangalore international short film festival and Goa short film carnival.
His Ad 'RajmaUpma' won the national level ad film competition 'CrashthePepsiIPL'
He has been conducting film making workshops across Bangalore for the last two years.
Has worked as an associate director for a Kannada feature film and currently working on his debut directorial venture in Kannada
He is an engineer turned film maker and hence follows an engineering approach in making and teaching films.
